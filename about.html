About Me

Front End Web Developer and Photographer located in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Just a few things about me.

After growing up in Montana, I went to school in Seattle and Los Angeles before earning a degree in Video Production. Later I worked in the Southern California production industry before transitioning into the tech industry working for companies such as Apple, Myspace and Twitter. My family and I moved to the Bay Area in 2014.

In my free time I enjoy photography, traveling, backpacking, yoga, gaming, trivia and following extreme sports like auto racing and high altitude mountaineering. I also deeply cherish spending time with my wife and 1 year old daughter.
